The opacity of the red tile represents how probable it is to be landed on in a given scenario, the more transparent the red tile is the less probable the space is to be landed on. The steady state probability is the probability that a player is on that tile at infinite turns. Clicking on a tile will show the probability of where you will land after the amount of turns in the depth box below (defaults to 1).
